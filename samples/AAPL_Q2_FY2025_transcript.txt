Apple Inc. Q2 FY2025 Earnings Call Transcript
Date: April 27, 2025

OPERATOR: Good day, and welcome to Apple's Q2 FY2025 Earnings Conference Call.

TIM COOK, CEO: Thank you. Good afternoon, everyone. Today, we're reporting another record quarter with revenue of $97.3 billion, up 5% year over year. 

Our iPhone revenue grew 7% driven by strong demand for the iPhone 16 Pro lineup. Services revenue reached an all-time high of $23.1 billion, up 12% year over year. 

Gross margin came in at 45.2%, up 80 basis points from last year, reflecting favorable mix and operational efficiencies.

Looking ahead, we remain confident in our long-term growth prospects.

LUCA MAESTRI, CFO: Thank you, Tim. Revenue was $97.3 billion, above the high end of our guidance range. iPhone revenue was $51.3 billion, up 7% year over year.

Looking ahead to Q3, we expect revenue to grow in the low-to-mid single digits year over year. We expect gross margin between 44.5% and 45.5%.

OPERATOR: We'll now open the call for questions.

ANALYST: Can you provide more color on the iPhone 16 cycle?

TIM COOK, CEO: The iPhone 16 has been very well received. We're seeing strong adoption of our Pro models, which now represent over 40% of our mix.
